The Plan for v2.0

Everything we brainstormed, sorted into what we're building next vs. later.

🔨 Phase 1 — Building Next

The core upgrades that make the site actually work well day-to-day.

Next

Shopping Cart

Add items from the menu, see everything in your cart, change quantities, remove things, add special instructions per item, and see a running total. (Total is informational — payment is still Venmo/cash.)

Next

Admin Tools

An easy way for Mom & Millie to update what's available — no code needed. A Google Sheet feeds the menu directly.

Deciding

Photo Hosting for the Sheet

How photos get into that Google Sheet menu — leaning toward Google Drive links (simple, free), with Cloudinary as a sturdier option later.

Next

Pickup Time Slots

Pick from real available windows instead of typing a preferred time — keeps orders manageable.

Next

Order Scheduling

A real order-by / pickup cadence (like "order by Thursday for weekend pickup") shown right on the site.

Next

Menu Category Filters

Quick filters (Cookies / Breads / Cakes) so browsing the menu is faster.

Next

Real Content

Swap placeholder photos for real ones, add the real Instagram handle, Venmo handle, and Formspree ID.

Next

Mobile Pass

Most visitors will be on their phones — give the whole site a real mobile-friendly check and polish.

🌱 Phase 2 — Later

Trust-building and polish, once the core is solid and there are real orders.

Later

Trust & FAQ

A FAQ (delivery area, lead time, allergen info) and a simple "How It Works" 1-2-3 strip so first-time visitors feel comfortable ordering.

Later

Reviews & Testimonials

Customer name, what they ordered, maybe a photo — needs real customers first, but it's a big trust builder.

Later

Customer Favorite Badges

Highlight top-selling items once we know what actually sells.

Later

Delivery Fee Transparency

If a delivery fee ever gets added, show it up front in the cart — never as a surprise at the end.

Later

Site Polish

A favicon/logo mark, a nice preview image for when the link is shared, and a background music toggle button.

Later

Meet the Baker

A personal bio section with photos of Millie & Mom — people like buying from people!

Deliberately skipping: customer accounts/login, live online payment (Stripe/Apple Pay), real-time order-tracking dashboards. That's the big-platform playbook (Uber Eats, etc.) — more complexity than this site needs. Formspree + Venmo/cash stays the plan.
Next step: Phase 1 turns into the v2.0 build prompt for Claude Code. Phase 2 waits until the core is live and working.
